LivePace Running App

LivePace is for runners who want to track their pace and share predicted finish times with supporters during a race. It records milestones manually – no GPS – keeping battery usage minimal.

Runners can set the target distance and expected minimum and maximum pace, as well as checkpoints (such as km markers or water breaks) along the way. They can track their progress and/or rest breaks, and the app will keep the predicted arrival times at checkpoints and the finish updated. They can share updates with supporters over any messaging app.

For more information, see the Features page or try the app out yourself
