LivePace Running App

LivePace is for runners who want to track their pace and share predicted finish times with supporters during a race. It records milestones manually – no GPS – keeping battery usage minimal.

Features
Runners can set the target distance and expected minimum and maximum pace, as well as checkpoints along the way. Then as they pass milestones (like km markers or water tables), or have rest breaks, they can quickly update their progress.
LivePace will keep track of the pace for each milestone, and allow them to share a message (over any messaging app) with supporters who may want to meet them along the way or congratulate them at the finish line.
